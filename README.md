# Netflix Trend Analysis by Release Year

## Project Overview

This project analyzes how Netflix content production has evolved over time using the Netflix dataset. The analysis focuses on yearly content releases, growth trends, decline periods, and business insights derived from historical patterns.

## Objective

The objective of this project is to examine Netflix's content production trends and understand how the number of released titles has changed over the years.


## Task Workflow

### Step 1: Organize Content by Release Year

Content was grouped according to the `release_year` column to identify the number of titles released each year.

### Step 2: Calculate Yearly Content Releases

The total number of movies and TV shows released each year was calculated using aggregation techniques.

### Step 3: Identify Growth and Decline Trends

Year-over-year changes and growth rates were analyzed to determine periods of expansion and decline.

### Step 4: Create Trend Visualizations

Several visualizations were created to better understand production trends:

* Line Chart
* Bar Chart
* Growth Rate Trend Chart

### Step 5: Interpret Business Patterns

The observed trends were interpreted from a business perspective to understand Netflix's content strategy.

## Key Findings

### Growth Phase

* Netflix content production increased significantly after 2010.
* Rapid growth was observed between 2015 and 2018.
* Peak production levels were reached around 2018.

### Decline Phase

* A decline in yearly releases was observed after 2018.
* Production slowed down during 2020 and 2021.
* Possible reasons include production disruptions, strategic shifts, and content optimization.

### Business Insights

Netflix followed an aggressive expansion strategy during the mid-2010s by investing heavily in original and international content. After reaching peak production levels, the company appears to have shifted toward a more selective and quality-focused content strategy.

## Conclusion

Netflix experienced substantial growth in content production over the years, followed by a gradual decline after reaching peak output. The analysis suggests a transition from rapid expansion to a more sustainable and strategic approach toward content creation.
